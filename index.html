About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto. I also work as a research scientist at Google DeepMind.
My research focuses on programming languages, in particular functional programming, type systems, logics, as well as their applications in the broader domains (e.g. compilers, code generation, and machine learning).

Prospective students: I'm actively looking for self-motivated students to join me at UofT. Please take a look at this link.
