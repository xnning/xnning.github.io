About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on programming languages, in particular functional programming, type systems, logics, as well as their applications in broader domains.
Previously, I was a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, where I currently hold a part-time position. Before that, I was a Research Associate at the University of Cambridge. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Hong Kong, and my B.S. from Zhejiang University.

Prospective students: I always look for motivated students to join my group. Please take a look at this link.
