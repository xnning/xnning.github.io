About

I will be on the job market in for 2022 positions.
I am an (incoming) research associate at University of Cambridge. I finished my Ph.D. in the Programming Languages Group at University of Hong Kong. Before that, I obtained my B.S. (with Honors) from Zhejiang Unversity.
I had the pleasure to work with a lot of amazing people during my Ph.D. journey. Check out my wonderful collaborators!
I am a former research intern at DeepMind London (remote), and a research intern at the RiSE team at Microsoft Research Redmond, and more previously a software engineer intern at Google Search in New York City.
I am a steering committee member of the Haskell Symposium.
